Neat little strategy featuring creative gameplay, immersive mechanics, and 2D pixel art visuals. Set up your troops, get into battle, and watch your glorious victory. Wide selection of troops allows for tons of possible tactics. Smart strategy along with devastating power will ensure your victory. Upgrade your technologies in order to always be one stage ahead of your opponent.


By upgrading your troops, you not only enhance their characteristics but also progress them through technological generations, which changes their appearance as well. Make your way from medieval era archers to modern age snipers! You, however, are not the only one developing your army;  enemies make technological advancements as well. It’s all in your hands to determine the winner of this arms race!


Each level requires a creative and precise approach in organizing the units for you to overcome it. With lots of possible tactics and win conditions, there is plenty of space for you to utilize your strategic genius. When even the smallest adjustment to conformation may lead to a drastically different outcome, a deep understanding of your strengths and weaknesses, as well as the great degree of providence is required.
A stylized visuals help you dive into this immersive gameplay. The pixel art style brings a very satisfactory image without sacrificing performance, which allows for incredibly spectacular large-scale battles. An overall 2D look of the game blends perfectly together with pixel art graphics.



• Upgrade System - Each troop has its own upgrade path, which pushes him through technological advancements. With each new generation, units change their appearance, properties and, obviously, strength.

• Diverse Amount Of Troops - With that many units, each having their own strengths, weaknesses and purpose, there are tons of options in customizing your strategy. You can go with a well-balanced army or focus on its one particular component; any option is great until it brings you victory.

• Great Strategic Core - Each and every adjustment you make affects the outcome of the battle. Use your brain and pure strategic skill in order to defeat your opponent.

• Large-Scale Battles - With hundreds of warriors on  screen, fights get really spectacular.

• Stylized Graphics - The visual component of this game is represented by 2d pixel art, which brings a lot of appeal towards the visuals.


My way in game development has started in 2020, back then I've been practicing pixel art, at some point, decided to give it a shot - to try myself in gamedev. Took a while, but on an august 4th, the result of all that work was ready for being published. And yes, it was this game - Grow Army.

After a while, I've gotten down to work on an other, more ambitious project. And thus the stylish bullet-hell, Aka my second game called Bullets Needed! was made. However, that's a completely different story.

Nevertheless, after more than a year since its initial release, Grow Army got an update, where I, using my newly acquired knowledge and experience, polished the old mechanics and introduced the new. I really do believe in games made with passion in mind, rather than monetization.
